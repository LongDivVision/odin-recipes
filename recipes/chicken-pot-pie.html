  1. Prepare the crust dough
    1. Set up dough mixer or food processor and equip it with dough mixer
    2. Measure out 2.5 cups of flour and add to the food processor
    3. add two sticks of Butter
    4. Add 10 oz buttermilk
    5. Add 1 tbsp of salt
    6. Add 1 tbsp of baking soda
    7. Add 2 tbsp of honey
    8. Mix on low until all ingredients are incorporated
    9. Add 2 eggs and mix on low speed until all ingredients are incorporated
    10. Set aside the dough in a plastic bag and refrigerate
  2. Create Pie Filling
    1. On the stove warm up a medium sized pot on medium low
    2. Fill the pot with the frozen mixed vegatables
    3. Allow vegetables to thaw in the pot
    4. Add 1 tbs crushed black pepper
    5. Add 1 tsp himalayan pink salt
    6. Once thawed remove from heat
  3. The Chicken
    1. Add small pot to the stove on medium heat
    2. Add cans of chicken
    3. Add 1/2cup chicken stock
    4. simmer over medium heat for 7 minutes or until stock has reduced by half
  4. Add everything to a pan
  5. Bake on 375 degrees for 60 minutes or until crust is flakey and golden brown
